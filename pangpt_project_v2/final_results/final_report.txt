====================================================================================================
PHASE-BY-PHASE ANALYSIS
====================================================================================================

PHASE 1: BASELINE (Weak Regularization)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Configuration:
  • Architecture: 6 layers, 512 embed dim, 8 heads (~50M params)
  • Regularization: Minimal (dropout=0.1, no label smoothing)
  • Training time: ~6 hours

Results:
  • Validation accuracy: 65.5%
  • Test accuracy: 0.51%
  • Test perplexity: 2.49

Analysis:
  ❌ SEVERE OVERFITTING: 65.5% → 0.51% (gap of 65%)
  ❌ Model memorized training data but couldn't generalize
  ❌ Test accuracy essentially random

PHASE 2: STRONG REGULARIZATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Configuration:
  • Architecture: 6 layers, 256 embed dim, 8 heads (~30M params)
  • Regularization: Strong (dropout=0.2, label_smoothing=0.1, weight_decay=0.001)
  • Training time: ~6 hours

Results:
  • Validation accuracy: 50.28%
  • Test accuracy: 50.23%
  • Test perplexity: 258.32

Analysis:
  ❌ TOO MUCH REGULARIZATION: Model couldn't learn
  ✅ No overfitting (val ≈ test)
  ⚠️  Accuracy stuck at 50% plateau

PHASE 2A: MODERATE REGULARIZATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Configuration:
  • Architecture: 6 layers, 256 embed dim, 8 heads (~30M params)
  • Regularization: Moderate (dropout=0.15, label_smoothing=0.05, weight_decay=0.0005)
  • Training time: ~6 hours

Results:
  • Validation accuracy: 50.27%
  • Test accuracy: 50.27%
  • Test perplexity: 142.32

Analysis:
  ✅ BALANCED: No overfitting, stable performance
  ✅ Best perplexity among 50% plateau models
  ⚠️  Still stuck at 50% plateau

PHASE 3: DEEP MODEL (3x Larger)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Configuration:
  • Architecture: 12 layers, 512 embed dim, 16 heads (~89M params)
  • Regularization: Moderate (same as Phase 2A)
  • Training time: ~27 hours (4.5x longer)

Results:
  • Validation accuracy: 50.29%
  • Test accuracy: 50.29%
  • Test perplexity: 141.30

Analysis:
  ❌ NO IMPROVEMENT: 3x more parameters, same 50% accuracy
  ❌ 4.5x longer training, 0.02% improvement (negligible)
  ✅ Proves: Problem is NOT model capacity
